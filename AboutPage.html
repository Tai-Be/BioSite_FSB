About Toby

Born and raised in the vibrant heart of Morocco, Toby embodies the resilience and spirit of his homeland. At 19, he embarked on a solo journey that would reshape his physical being and forge a path of self-discovery and transformation. Landing in the US right before the pandemic, Toby found himself in a new geographical and personal landscape. Weighing 205 pounds and longing for a change, he made a decisive choice. Instead of succumbing to isolation and uncertainty, Toby embraced the unknown and rewrote his own narrative.

Toby's weapon of choice was not a weapon nor a shield but a tennis racket, gym weights, and an unbreakable resolve. His days turned into a mix of hard work and believing in himself. The sound of tennis balls hitting the court, the ache of muscles during gym sessions, and the soothing feeling of gliding through the water during his regular swim laps became Toby's way of finding peace and strength. Six months. That's all it took for Toby to shed nearly 50 pounds and unveil a new version of himself. But the transformation went far beyond the numbers on the scale.
